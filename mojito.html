Mojito

Ingredients:

  • 1 Lime
  • Mint
  • 10g Cane Sugar
  • 4 Ice Cubes
  • Rum
  • carbonated water

Preparations:

  1. Cut the lime into slices and squeeze the juice into a glass. Put the slices in the glass as well.
  2. Add cane sugar and mint to the glass and crush with a pestle.
  3. Add the ice cubes and use the pestle to crush slightly.
  4. Add carbonated water and light rum.
  5. Top with cane sugar and mint.
