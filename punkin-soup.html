Pumpkin-Soup

Ingridients

  • 500g Hokkaido Pumpkin
  • 200g Sweet Potato
  • 200g Carrot
  • 1/2 Tin of Coconut Milk
  • 500ml vegetable broth
  • salt & pepper
  • muskat

Preperation

  • cut all the vegetables into small pieces
  • blend in all the vegetables together
  • add broth and coconut milk
  • cook for 40 minutes at medium temperatur
  • spice as you like with salt, pepper & muskat
